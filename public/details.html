Reduce Losses

Reduce the percentage of material losses in case of project failure through takatuf plan, which depends on several things:

The buyer pays the value of the product every month in an agreed percentage of the product price and does not exceed 10%

In case of payment of the amount within a specified period and a specified percentage, the product is owned by the buyer

In case of loss in the project, the existing payment period, regardless of its duration, is considered as a paid replacement for the product and is returned to the seller

By adhering to this mechanism, the material burden on the buyer can be alleviated through the payment mechanism described, and in case of failure of the project, losses can be mitigated through the mechanism of the amount paid during the period is considered a rental alternative, and with this plan, we have reduced the financial burden in case of profit and reduced the financial burden in case of loss
